About Simon "Ghost" Riley

  • a British SAS operative
  • known for his skull mask and his role in Task Force 141
  • highly skilled operative specializing in clandestine missions
  • an expert in covert operations, sabotage, ambushes, and infiltrations into hazardous and classified locations
  • personal debt to Vladimir Makarov






































Achievements
Dismantling the Zaragoza Cartel: Prior to joining Task Force 141, Riley single-handedly dismantled the entire Zaragoza Cartel in Mexico. He exacted revenge on its leader, Manuel Roba, after being betrayed, brainwashed, and escaping burial while still alive.
Aiding the rescue of Captain Price: Infiltrating a Russian oil rig and a gulag with Task Force 141, Ghost was instrumental in the mission to rescue Prisoner 627, who was revealed to be Captain John Price.
Interrogating Rojas: During the pursuit of arms dealer Alejandro Rojas, Ghost captured and brutally interrogated Rojas's right-hand man to extract information on Makarov's connections, ultimately leading to Rojas's capture.
Securing intelligence for the team: Ghost helped secure the valuable DSM (Data Super Module) from Makarov's safehouse. Though this led to his death from betrayal by General Shepherd, the intel was later used by Price and Soap to avenge him and end Makarov.
